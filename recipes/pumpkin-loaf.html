Pumpkin Loaf Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Combine your flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together your pumpkin puree, vegetable oil, sugar, and eggs.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Spoon the batter into a greased 9×5″ loaf pan and bake for about 45 minutes.

Enjoy!
